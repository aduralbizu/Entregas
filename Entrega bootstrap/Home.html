About us

Yonsei University has always followed the righteous path through the twists and turns of Korea’s modern history. As a distinguished global academic institution, Yonsei has made significant contributions to society and humanity. Inheriting this prestigious tradition, we aim to advance into a new era of Yonsei, thereby opening the gateway to the future with what makes us uniquely YONSEI.

In the age of “Big Blur," we are experiencing the collapse of boundaries between academia and industry. We are confronted with the dual challenges of digital transformation and energy transition on the fiercely competitive battlefield of innovation. Words like creativity, consilience, and convergence have long since become keywords in our society. Innovation is the only way to stay relevant in this rapidly changing international environment.

Yonsei's enduring mission for 139 years has been to nurture talents who drive innovation and change. We will continue to build on this foundation, to cultivate individuals who proactively tackle the problems of global society while pursuing the values of coexistence. We will strengthen programs that foster creative future talent, such as the “Student-Designed Semester,” enabling students to freely explore academia, to grow as independent agents of the future society.

Yonsei University is one of the oldest and most prestigious private universities in South Korea. Founded in 1885 by Christian missionaries, Yonsei is known for its strong Christian heritage and commitment to academic excellence. The university is located in Seoul, the capital city of South Korea, and has a sprawling campus known for its beautiful architecture and modern facilities.

Yonsei University offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs across various fields of study. The university is renowned for its rigorous academic standards, innovative teaching methods, and world-class faculty. Yonsei consistently ranks among the top universities in South Korea and is recognized internationally for its academic excellence and research output.

Yonsei University maintains partnerships with leading universities and institutions worldwide, facilitating academic exchanges, joint research projects, and international collaboration. The university encourages global citizenship and offers numerous opportunities for students to study abroad, participate in international conferences, and engage in cross-cultural experiences. Yonsei's diverse and multicultural community enriches the learning environment and prepares students for success in an increasingly interconnected world.
